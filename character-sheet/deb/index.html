Divine SparkAction · 30ft▶
Point Holy Symbol at a creature within 30ft. Roll 1d8+3. Either restore that many HP to the creature, OR force CON save DC 14 — fail takes Necrotic or Radiant damage equal to roll, success takes half.
Turn Undead + Sear UndeadAction · 30ft▶
Each Undead of your choice within 30ft makes WIS save DC 14. Fail: Frightened + Incapacitated for 1 minute, tries to flee. Bonus from Sear Undead: failing undead also take 3d8 Radiant damage immediately!
Guided StrikeReaction for ally · 30ft▶
When you or a creature within 30ft misses an attack, give that roll +10. Reaction required only to benefit an ally. Can turn a near-miss into a guaranteed hit — save for high-stakes attacks like a paladin's smite.
War God's Blessing ★ Best openerBonus Action▶
Cast Shield of Faith (+2 AC, 60ft) or Spiritual Weapon (floating +6 atk, 1d8+3 Force, moves each BA) using CD instead of a spell slot. KEY: no Concentration! Lasts 1 min. Use this to run Spiritual Weapon + Spirit Guardians simultaneously — they no longer compete. Optimal turn: BA War God's Blessing (Spiritual Weapon) → Action Spirit Guardians.

Guidance (cantrip)At will · Conc▶
Before any ability check: touch an ally, they add 1d4. Lockpicking, persuasion, climbing, investigation, performance — anything. Drop if combat starts. Use this ALL THE TIME out of combat. Highest value cantrip in the game.
Spare the Dying (cantrip)At will · 15ft▶
Stabilize any downed ally at 15ft instantly — no slot. Use this instead of Healing Word when someone just needs to not die. Save the BA heal for when they need to get back up and fight.
Healing Hands (Aasimar)1/Long Rest▶
Magic action: touch a creature, restore 3d4 HP. Completely free — no spell slot. Use every long rest without thinking. Great for topping someone off between fights.
Detect Magic (ritual)No slot if ritual▶
You have this prepared! Cast as a 10-minute ritual (no slot) to sense magical auras within 30ft. Use when exploring a new room, checking a suspicious object, or wondering if an NPC is enchanted. Never burn a slot — always ritual it.
Pre-combat buffs (cast BEFORE initiative)
Aid (2nd slot)8 hours · No Conc▶
Cast before entering a dungeon — +5 max HP for 3 allies lasts 8 hours with no concentration. Free buffer HP for the whole adventuring day. Easy to forget because it has no flashy effect.
Magic Weapon (2nd slot)1 hour · No Conc▶
Your Mace becomes a +1 magic weapon for an hour. Cast before combat — free +1 to all attack and damage rolls. No concentration needed.
Bless (1st slot)Round 1 priority▶
Cast FIRST THING every significant combat. +d4 to attack rolls AND saving throws for 3 allies for 1 min. Also helps resist charm, frighten, and paralysis. Conflicts with Spirit Guardians for concentration — but if you use War God's Blessing for Spiritual Weapon (no Conc), you can still run Bless as your concentration spell!
Channel Divinity smart plays
Optimal combat openerBest combo▶
Option A (max damage): BA War God's Blessing → Spiritual Weapon (no Conc, free) · Action → Spirit Guardians (Conc) · now both are running!

Option B (buff team): BA War God's Blessing → Shield of Faith on squishy ally (no Conc) · Action → Bless (Conc, +d4 for 3 allies)
Guided Strike timing▶
+10 to a missed roll sounds good but save it for genuinely high-stakes moments — a paladin's smite, a rogue's sneak attack, your own Inflict Wounds on a boss. Don't blow it on a regular sword swing.
Social & exploration skills to remember
Insight +6 (passive 16)▶
Your passive Insight is 16 — the DM may tell you when someone is lying without you asking. Actively roll Insight during negotiations, interrogations, or when a story seems off. Your +6 is excellent.
Intimidation +4▶
Divine warrior with celestial powers — leaning on Intimidation is very on-brand. Ask your DM if activating Celestial Revelation (glowing eyes, wings) gives Advantage on Intimidation checks. Might just end a fight before it starts.
Medicine +3▶
Diagnose illness or poison (DC 10-15), stabilize dying creatures, determine cause of death. Check Medicine before burning a Lesser Restoration slot — you might not need the spell.
Lesser Restoration timing▶
Only a Bonus Action in 2024! When an ally is Blinded, Paralyzed, or Poisoned, removing that condition is often worth more than any damage you could deal. You can remove the condition AND still attack on the same turn.
